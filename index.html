DeVAULT WILDLIFE LAB

at University of Georgia—Savannah River Ecology Laboratory

Our Interests

We apply interdisciplinary field and laboratory approaches to develop science-based solutions to wildlife management and conservation. Although much of our research is focused on carnivores, we work on a diversity of other taxa including birds, wild pigs, small mammals, and invertebrates.
